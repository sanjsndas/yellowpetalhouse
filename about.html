    Yellow Petal House is a living library of gardening knowledge — crafted by growers, for growers.

    Founded in 2016, we've grown from a small community blog into a trusted resource for both beginners and seasoned horticulturists. Our roots are in organic practice, ecological design, and the simple joy of watching things grow.

    Our guiding principles

    Ecological stewardship

    We champion practices that regenerate soil, support pollinators, and honor natural cycles.

    Evidence-based growing

    Every guide is rooted in horticultural science, field trials, and centuries of traditional knowledge.

    Gardening for all

    From balcony pots to acreage, we meet you where you are — with clarity and encouragement.
