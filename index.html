    Frequently asked questions

    How often should I amend my soil?

    Test annually; add compost and organic matter in spring and fall. Adjust based on crop needs.

    What are the best perennials for partial shade?

    Hellebores, ferns, astilbe, and hakone grass thrive with dappled light.

    Can I grow vegetables in small containers?

    Absolutely. Dwarf tomatoes, peppers, and salad greens perform well in 5-gallon pots.
